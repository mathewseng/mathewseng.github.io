Ultimate Omaha Rules

Gameplay

  1. Each player places an equal bet and receives 4 hole cards
  2. Preflop: Check or Double your bet
  3. Two separate flops are revealed
  4. Flop: Check or Double again
  5. Showdown: Turns and Rivers are revealed

Qualifying

You must make a Pair of Aces or better on BOTH boards to qualify.

If you don't qualify, you foul and lose your bet.
